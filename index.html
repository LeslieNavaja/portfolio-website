About me

I am Leslie Faith D. Navaja, a dynamic and adaptable individual with a passion for technology and web development. Born on May 6, 2004, in Botolan, Zambales, I have grown into a versatile professional, always eager to engage in whatever activities life brings my way.
I work part-time with my sister, a professional website developer, as a webpage filler and encoder. In this role, I ensure that content is accurately inputted and displayed on various websites, which has provided me with a solid foundation in web development and content management. Additionally, I enjoy using Figma for website-focused projects, leveraging its intuitive design tools to bring creative concepts to life and contribute to the design process meaningfully.

Beyond my encoding responsibilities, I have also taken on webpage creation tasks, expanding my practical skills in building and maintaining websites. My experiences have honed my abilities in web development, content management, and design tools.

I believe in embracing life as it comes, which allows me to stay adaptable and open to new challenges and opportunities. This mindset ensures continuous personal and professional growth, making me always ready to learn and evolve in the ever-changing world of technology and web development.
